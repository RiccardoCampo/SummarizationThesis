On the Road in America , 12 part reality series currently being broadcast throughout Middle East , features young Arabs crisscrossing America on mission to educate themselves and people they encounter along way ; production credits reveal involvement by Bush administration , former Pres George H W Bush and slew of prominent members of former Republican and Democrat administrations ; this unlikely coalition of unpaid consultants , whose principal role was to raise money and to knock down diplomatic doors , has helped create series primarily intended to reintroduce America to Arab world through new eyes ; photos ( M ).
